Design System

Images

Images are essential part of web. They need to be responsive so they won't get bigger than their parent container. Use our predefined classes to make images responsive as well as of custom sizes.

Square Images

They are of Square shaped with dimensions equal to 200 x 200.

Round Images

These are cicular images with a dimension of 200 x 200.

Responsive Images

These images will be as big as their parent container to give them responsive nature. As their parent grows and shrink they will also grow and shrink.
